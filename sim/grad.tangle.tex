\subsection{Calculation of the torsional angles gradient}

The torsional angles part reads
\begin{equation}
  \Hta=\sum_{i=2}^{N-2} C(i)(1+\cos\phi_i)+D(i)(1+\cos{3\phi_i}).
  \label{ta}
\end{equation}
where
\begin{equation}
\begin{cases}
 C(i)=0,\quad D(i)=0.2\\
 C(i)=D(i)=1.2
\end{cases}
  \label{def-cd}
\end{equation}
